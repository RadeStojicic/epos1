About Us

About Pixi Barbershop

❛The Beggining

We are third generation barbers with a high level experience and expertise in all aspects of our craft. The reason to our success is the overall experience we have working and learning from a diverse culture. We are committed and determined to make all our clients happy through listening and suggesting the best style that fits you the most. We are about substance and skill, not gimmicks. We like to have fun, but we take our work seriously. Our team is highly skilled and fluent in all of the latest trends, hair cutting, trimming, and shaving methods. We want you looking and feeling your best, plain and simple. That means we only use the best products. We’ll always go out of our way to meet your needs; just ask any staff member, and expect immediate service.

Our mission❜

Treat yourself to something better. Come in and experience the difference of superior barbering and grooming at our shop. We concede that a person’s look must correlate with their image and style. Our experienced group of professional stylists will guide you into crafting the appearance of your choice. Our variety of dynamic barbers can suggest a variety of different styles. We will walk you through every way of the process from uncovering the proper shape, to coinciding the covent features of your face to match your attributes, we advise of what you can emphasize on in order to achieve a like minded goal which is to discover what suits you the best.
